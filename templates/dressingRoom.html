Set up your webcam and check your hair. When all looks good, click the green button to start cooking.

Video Tips

  • For best video quality, upgrade to Flash 11. Right-click (PC) or control-click (Mac) on the box to the left to find out your version. You can get Flash 11 here.
  • If video isn't showing, click the settings icon and select the right camera. You can also adjust the volume.
  • If all else fails, try refreshing the page.
