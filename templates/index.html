Cooking = awesome

What's better than a homemade meal, with the freshest seasonal ingredients, hand-crafted by you? How about making it together with friends? Turn up the flame and roll up your sleeves. Let's cook!

Cook with anyone, anywhere

No matter where you are, you can be in each other’s kitchens, so you can co-create the masterpiece that will be dinner. Show off your knife skills—or ask for pro tips from your buddy. You can even jot down notes together.

A recipe for every palate

Can't decide what to cook? We did the hard work for you: choose from our curated collection of meals, all personally vetted in our kitchens for deliciousness and makeability, as well as healthfulness and seasonality. Leave the hours of searching and testing to us, so you can do what's fun: cook!

Revisit the good times

Hotpot keeps a record of every time you cook, so you can see the things you’ve made over time, revisit your cooking notes, and relive the excitement of that one time your salmon burst into flames.

Coming soon: the best community

We're building not just the best community of cooks, but of awesome people from all walks of life. Meet new friends the world over, swap food stories, and of course, cook together!
